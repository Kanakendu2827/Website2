BLOOD DONATION

Mariners Onside Blood Donation Camp was a meaningful initiative driven by compassion, responsibility, and community spirit. Organized with the belief that true support goes beyond the stands, the camp aimed to give back to society by helping those in need of life-saving blood. Members and well-wishers came forward wholeheartedly, turning solidarity into action and passion into purpose. The initiative highlighted Mariners Onside’s commitment to social responsibility, proving that being a supporter is not only about cheering for the team, but also about standing together for humanity. Through this noble effort, Mariners Onside once again showed that the green-and-maroon spirit beats strongest when it serves others.
